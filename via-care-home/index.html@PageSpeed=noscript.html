Introduction

A great homecoming for you is our goal! QS General Insurance provides “Home Trip ViaCare” for the expatriates of India with an option to conveniently purchase their home trip travel insurance online when travelling to India. It offers the expatriate(s), who travel to India to visit their family, friends or on business, an insurance coverage for hospitalization, medical expenses, accidental death, permanent disability and other travel inconveniences.
